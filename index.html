The Cascade of Hope Community Initiative is registered as a Community Based Organization committed to improving the lives of less fortunate in Ruai Ward, Kasarani Sub County, and Nairobi County Kenya. The area served by the Organization is characterized by high levels of poverty, deplorable dwelling structures, inadequate sanitation facilities and general despondency and hopelessness due to high unemployment rates. All these conditions predispose residents to all kinds of diseases including TB/HIV/AIDS. The Health Center and community based were born out of the desire and compassion to reach out and reconstruct slums dwellers living in abject poverty, hopelessness, despair and mental health

Health Service Delivery

The Cascade of Hope Community Health Centre is a wing of social development within Cascade of Hope Community Initiative. It runs A wide range of health services with the goal of promoting universals access to quality healthcare.

  • HIV&AIDS prevention, treatment, Orphans and Vulnerable Children support, mitigation of Sexual and Gender Based Violence and Stigma

  • Tuberculosis (TB) diagnosis and treatment, defaulter and contact tracing and TB/HIV co-infection management

  • COVID-19 prevention and dissemination of massages of hope.

  • Maternal, neonatal and child health services

  • Reproductive Health and Family Planning
  • Hypertension education and screening at community level and referral linkages for treatment
  • Diabetes awareness, screening and management.
  • Hospital Quality Improvement Program.

Our Approach

We work alongside the communities we serve with local organizations because we know that a locally owned approach is the best way to deliver care where it is needed most and help people stay healthy.
